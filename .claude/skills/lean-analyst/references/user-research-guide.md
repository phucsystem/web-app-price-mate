## Validation Methods

| Method | Best For | Effort | Confidence |
|--------|----------|--------|------------|
| **User Interviews** | Problem discovery | 5-10 interviews | HIGH |
| **Surveys** | Quantitative validation | 100+ responses | MEDIUM |
| **Landing Page** | Interest validation | 100+ visitors | MEDIUM |
| **Prototype Testing** | Usability | 5 users | HIGH |
| **Concierge MVP** | Solution validation | 5-10 customers | VERY HIGH |

## Interview Script Template

```markdown
**Warm-up (5 min):**
- Role and daily workflow

**Problem Discovery (10 min):**
- How do you currently [task]?
- What's frustrating?
- Walk me through last time you [experienced problem]

**Solution Validation (10 min):**
- [Show mockup] What does this do?
- How would you use this?
- What's missing?

**Wrap-up (5 min):**
- Would you use this? Why?
- What would make you switch?
```
